Project plan

Title

Sultan Fire Department

Goal

The goals are:

  • Replace old and out of date site
  • Educate and provide info to the concerned public about department operations

Target Audience

  • Residents within the fire district
  • prosepctive volunteers and employeees
  • people looking for information on yard waste burning and current burn ban info
  • People looking for information the departments operations and programs offered
    • fire explorers
    • CPR and first aid training
    • youth and adult public fire safety education
    • information about upcoming department events and involvement in community events
  • Fire deparment enthusiasts

Main Features

Stuff I want to include on the site:

  • A recrtuing ad. The department is rural and relies heavily on volunteers and part time staff and because of this departemnts experince and training, the part-time and volunteer staff are constantly being hired away to full time jobs in larger departments. This might need to be included in the recruitment.
  • Current events and news, a feed of some kind. The city has a facebook page where citizens are constatly asking "what are these sirens about?" and "where can i get info about the Fire station hosting a birthday party?" This could be their one stop shop.
  • Current events and news, perhaps live social media windows or views (twitter or facebook)
  • Nav. Want to keep this simple yet informatative. Home, about, public education, CPR/First aid calsses and perhaps a sign-up. Contact info at least.
  • Pictures of memebers in action, pictures of the vehicles. Familiarity is a comfort thing, citizens and also Firefighting buffs (see: nerds) can also enjoy looking at and learning about the vehicles and equipment
  • embeded live radio scanner

Personality

The mood is Bold, traditional, pride, helpful

Visitors will feel informed about operations and feel pride in the department

Limiting Factors

There will be quite a few images that may take up extra bandwidth. Some internet experience is needed to navigate the site.

Development guide

Site structure

  • Root Directory
    • images directory – contains jpgs and gifs
    • styles directory – contains all of the style sheets
    • scripts directory - contains the nav or other js scripts
    • index.html – home page for site, has news postings, burn ban info, nav, recruitment add, scanner embed, and contact in footer.
    • about-us.html - will feature department history, response stats, leadership structure, response area map.
    • recruitment.html - will describe membership types, candidate interview and training process, how to apply.
    • fleet-station.html - featuring the station and units housed there. short descriptions and pictures of each unit. each unit will have it's own page.
    • contact-us.html - mailing address and email contact options. also fb messenger.
    • faq.html - list of questions we often see and answers, as well as contant options
