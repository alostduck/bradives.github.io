About Utility 51

Utility 51 is a 4x4 chevy ambulance converted to be a light rescue unit. This unit is almost exclusively with and by our swift water rescue team. It contains tools for rope systems and rescue as well as equipment needed for water rescue operations such as floating rope, buoys, water proof bags for our radios and a stokes basket for hauling patients via rope system or if the person needs to be carried a long distance (for example down a hiking trail) the basket has a large wheel that can be attached to the bottom. This unit can also double as an aid unit if needed, and has been used in snowy conditions due to its ability to be used as a 4x4.

Tools carried on U-51 include:

  • Many different types of rope for rope rescue systems
  • medical equipment
  • tools to support the swift water rescue team
