About Aid 51

Aid 51 is a Braun ambulance mounted on a Ford E350 van chasse. This is our first out aid unit and is used for nearly all of our aid calls. The vehicle is typically staffed with 2 firefighter/EMTs but sometimes one of our probationary firefighters will be riding along as a 3rd crew member for training and evaluation as part of their progression as new EMTs. 80% of our 911 dispatches are medical calls so this vehicle is defiantly our busiest vehicle.

Equipment and Functions

  • medical equipment for evaluation and care
  • Self contained breathing apparatus (SCBA) and hand tools for crew to use in firefighting operations, as this unit can also respond to fires as needed.
  • Equipment to move people such as a stair-chair with tracks on it and foldable seats and tarps to move patients in.
