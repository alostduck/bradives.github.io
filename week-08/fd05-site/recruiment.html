Interested in becoming a firefighter?

Volunteer and part-time firefighters make up the majority of our staffing. We rely on these members to provide our constant, prompt and professional service levels to our community. Being a part-time or volunteer firefighter/EMT at Sultan Fire is a rewarding experience with a lot of valueable experience. No matter if you are able to work as a volunteer or part-time, we have oppertunity for everyone. Sultan has had a tradition of dedicated volunteer members since our beginning in 1911. Today, Part time and volunteer staffing makes half of our daytime shift and all of our night shift. Are you ready to Join us? Apply today!!!

Benefits to working at Sultan Fire

There are many benefits to volunteering or working with us part-time!

  • Free Training. Academy and EMT school is provided. As a Sultan firefighter, you will have an oppertunity to earn your Firefighter 1 and 2, Hazerdious materials awareness and operations, and National Regestiry Emergency Medical Technician.
  • All gear, uniforms and equipment will be provided!
  • Valuable field experience! we average 1200 calls a year and our community is growing!
  • Experience working in a family like atmosphere and form life long friendships! Our members work closely together and the work enviroment is like no other.

Requirements for working at Sultan Fire

  • Be at least 18 years old
  • Have earned a high school diploma or high school equivalency certificate.
  • Possess a valid Drivers License
  • pass both background and driving record checks
  • Be able to pass our physical agility or have a valid CPAT card
  • Complete and pass a medical physical and have immunizations
  • Once hired, complete all requirements of a probationary firefighter/EMT
  • Complete our application and mail/turn it in (our staff is always willing to accept completed applications in person!)
