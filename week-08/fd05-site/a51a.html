About Aid 51 Adam

Much like Engine 51 Adam is the back up to Engine 51, Aid 51 Adam is the backup to Aid 51. However, this unit does see a bit more action, so it is more like our second out Aid unit, meaning if Aid 51 is already on a call and there is a crew available, this unit will respond in place of a mutual aid unit coming from Monroe or Gold Bar. The inventory is nearly identical to A-51 with some slight differences such as equipment brands.

Equipment and Functions

  • medical equipment for evaluation and care
  • Self contained breathing apparatus (SCBA) and hand tools for crew to use in firefighting operations, as this unit can also respond to fires as needed.
  • Equipment to move people such as a stair-chair with tracks on it and foldable seats and tarps to move patients in.
