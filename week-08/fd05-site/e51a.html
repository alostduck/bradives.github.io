Engine 51 Adam


The Back Up Engine

The second out engine

Engine 51 adam is a 2002 HME pumper class engine. It carries much of the same tools and equipment that Engine 51 carries. It is used as a second out engine as well as a reserve/back up incase Engine 51 needs to be taken out of service for any reason such as repairs or maintenance.

Some differences between E51 and E51-A

  • E51-A carries 1000 gallons of water
  • E51-A has an electronic pump panel that is located on the front of the engine compared to E-51, where the pump panel is located on the drivers side with a half electronic, half mechanical pump panel.
  • Slightly different layout for tools and hoses and hose connections.
