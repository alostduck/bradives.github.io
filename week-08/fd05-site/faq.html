Questions we are asked often

Have a question for us? We are always happy to answer question's you may have for us as best as we can. Please feel free to call us at (360) 793-1179. This list is simply for quick reference.

  • Is there a burn ban on?
    You can always find up to date county wide burn ban information at The Snohomish county outdoor burning information page of the Snohomish county fire marshals office. We do not issue burn permits in the district and generally outdoor burning is not allowed without a permit.
