  • Free blood pressure checks!!

    The department is offering free blood-pressure checks! Feel free to stop and any one of our qualified firefighter/EMT's check your blood pressure during normal buisness hours (8 AM to 8 PM). It's free and you don't need a pre-existing condition. In fact, checking your blood-pressure may be an early warning aganist devloping conditions invloving blood pressure!! Come see us!

Interested in becoming a firefighter?

Sultan Fire is dependant on our part-time and volunteer Firefighter/EMT's. They work alongside our full time Firefighters to form the staffing needed to effectivly respond to our community. Working as a volunteer or part-time firefighter/EMT is a very rewarding experience for those interested in persuing a career in the fire service and gaining experience in this field or simply looking to serve their community!
