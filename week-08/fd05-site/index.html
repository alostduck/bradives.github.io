Current Burn Ban Status:

Currently there is no burn ban. For county-wide burning information and regulation please visit the Snohomish county fire marshal's office.

Interested in becoming a firefighter?

Sultan Fire is dependent on our part-time and volunteer Firefighter/EMTs. They work alongside our full-time Firefighters to form the staffing needed to effectively respond to our community. Working as a volunteer or part-time firefighter/EMT is a very rewarding experience for those interested in perusing a career in the fire service and gaining experience in this field or simply looking to serve their community! Department News

  • Free blood pressure checks and station hours

    Station hours are 9 AM to 8 PM daily

    The department is offering free blood pressure checks! Any one of our qualified firefighter/EMTs can check your blood pressure. Feel free to stop by the station during normal business hours. It's free and you don't need a pre-existing condition. In fact, checking your blood pressure may be an early warning against developing dangerous health conditions. Come see us!
