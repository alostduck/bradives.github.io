About Station 51

Station 51 is our only station, it houses Engine 51, Engine 51-A (adam, or backup), Tender-51, and the two aid units, Aid 51 and Aid 51-A (adam or backup). Additionally, units can be parked outside the station and are rotated out on a seasonal basis, these units are the Brush engines, Brush 51 and Brush 52 (the off road and back up brush engine) The utility (Utility 51) as well as the pick up, utility 51-A(adam), however this unit also doubles as a command unit, typically for one of the deputy chiefs. The command units (Chief 51, Battalion 51 and Deputy Chief 51) are kept with those officers so they may respond from anywhere.

What goes on at station 51?

Staffing

On a typical day, Station 51 is staffed by a combined staff of full time, part time and volunteer staffing. Our shifts are 12 hours long, however some of our part-time and volunteer staff combines those shifts into 24 hour days During the day, staffing is 1 full-time officer/firefighter/EMT, 1 full-time firefighter/EMT and 1-3 part-time or volunteer firefight/EMTs overseen by a duty chief. At night the station is staffed by 3-5 part-time firefighter/EMTs overseen by a designated duty chief.

Daily activity

On a daily basis, crews perform readiness checks on the units and equipment, have a daily workout and perform public outreach, education. Crews also complete training and drills.

Is the station staffed at night?

Yes it is! Due to our station's age and ordinal construction design, crews are unable to sleep within the actual station. This is being corrected with our new station, scheduled to begin construction in 2020.

While at night, the station may appear to be unmanned, the crews are indeed available to respond to calls, starting in 2018, the department was able to secure a sleeping residence for crews just behind the station, on Main street, next to city call. This greatly improved our night time responses.

New Station update

The department is going ahead with the new station 51, to be located at our current administration, located at 33019 Cascade View Dr, Sultan, WA 98294. The funding phase is now over (Thank you for your votes!) and the plans are being finalized, we hope to break ground and begin construction early in 2020.
