About Engine 51

Engine 51 is 2015 Peirce Impel 1500 GPM pumper class Engine that the department purchased brand new. It has seating for 6 fully geared firefighters, carries 750 gallons of water and includes some state of the art features such as a remote controlled top mounted master stream and a self managing pump. E51 is capable of driving at low speeds while being it's pump is active, making it useful for forestry operations.

Tools carried on E-51 include:

  • auto extrication tools (hydraulically powered spreaders also know as the "jaws of life" and cutters as well as an extending ram)
  • multiple hose configurations designed for attacking fire as well as defending nearby structures. Also forest fires.
  • hand tools and saws.
  • medical supplies
