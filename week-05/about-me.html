Connor the Firefighter

My oldest boy and middle child, Connor is featured in this picture doing one of his most favorate things in the world, driving daddy's fire truck.

He is sitting in the engineers seat of Engine 51 adam (E-51A), the back-up pumper of Sultan Fire Department which is one of two fire departments that I work at part time.

I chose this picture for a couple reasons, foremost being the fact that the boy a handsome little guy. Look at him!! Cute little fella. Also shows a bit of my life and since this is an about me page that makes sense. I'm a father and a firefighter and here is my boy and one of the fire engines I work off of.

E51-A is an older truck, a 2002 HME western states pumper, carrying 1000 gallons of water onboard as well as a 30 gallon foam tank. It has an eletronic pump panel that isn't without it's ghosts. It's not uncommon for it to randomly devlop a fault or have a valve get stuck open. Which is why I prefer the manual action pump panel of our first out engine, E-51. A much newer panel, it still has automatic functions but the valves are controlled by levers instead of switches.

Connor is a younger boy, a 2017 2 year old (at the time of this writing) little guy with a hearty laugh and a happy personality. he does not carry any water onboard but can have a few "external ounces of fluid" if a diaper change is needed. He runs off waffles, "chick nuggests" and "bu-nanas". It is not uncommon to see connor laughing, however he is known for his cuddles as well. Connor loves fire trucks but is always asking to go see daddies fire truck, which is where you see him here.
